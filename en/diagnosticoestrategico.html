Service 01

Strategic Visual Diagnosis

Clarity before design, production or costly mistakes.

If your project still lacks a clear visual direction, this service helps organize the bigger picture before moving into design or execution.

Start Your Project

What it is and what it helps define

A strategic analysis service designed to define the visual direction of a project before major design, production or budget decisions are made.

This is not a decorative stage or a brainstorming exercise. It is a structured reading of the project to identify what it needs, what remains unresolved and what should be defined before moving forward.

It brings strategic clarity before design or production, so the project can move forward with a clear visual direction instead of improvisation.

Ideal for

•

Production teams in pre-production

•

Projects that still feel visually undefined or disorganized

•

Teams that need aligned visual criteria before moving into design

•

Sets or spaces that do not yet have a clear direction

•

Projects where execution should not begin until the visual approach is properly understood

What it solves

  • Identifies what the project actually needs on a visual level
  • Reveals gaps, contradictions and premature decisions
  • Defines a conceptual base before moving into development
  • Helps avoid wasting time and budget on a poorly framed design approach

Included phases

Phase 0

Strategic project reading

Initial analysis to map the project context, visual intent and key unresolved points before design decisions are made.

Phase 1

Foundational visual direction

Definition of base criteria to guide creative direction, production planning and early project development with clarity.

What’s included

World statement
Guiding keywords
Conceptual moodboard
Base color system
Analysis of up to 3 proposed sets
Identification of visual and strategic risks

Deliverable

You will receive a PDF document with the project’s core visual direction, along with an explanatory video to clarify the key criteria, findings and recommendations.

Strategic PDF + explanatory video

What’s not included

Technical design
Plans
Full set development
Purchasing
Supervision
On-site production execution

This service does not replace a full design phase or complete art direction. Its role is to give the project clarity before moving into those stages.

Limits and revisions

Up to 3 sets reviewed
1 revision round
If the project later needs structural development or execution, it should move into the corresponding service.

Result

A project with a clear visual direction, defined criteria and a stronger foundation for deciding whether to move forward into design, refine the approach or correct the path before production.

This service is right for you if...

Good fit if

  • you do not yet have enough clarity to design properly
  • the project still feels visually disorganized
  • you need to define the intent before moving into production
  • you want to avoid improvised decisions from the start

Not the right fit if

  • you already need technical plans and execution-ready deliverables
  • you are looking for purchasing, installation or on-set supervision
  • what you need is not diagnosis, but full art direction or production design

Before you design anything, you need to understand what is actually being built.

If your project still needs direction, clarity and visual criteria before moving into a bigger stage, this is the right place to start.
